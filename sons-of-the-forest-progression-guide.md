# Sons Of The Forest Efficient Progression Checklist

This route assumes you want fast story progression while also collecting every optional-but-useful item: practical tools, weapons, attachments, mobility items, survival utilities, key blueprints, and major upgrades. It skips lore-only papers and mostly cosmetic outfits unless they are next to useful loot.

Core progression chain: **Rebreather -> Rope Gun -> Shovel -> Maintenance Keycard -> VIP Keycard -> Guest Keycard -> Residential Bunker -> Ancient Armor + Solafite -> Luxury Bunker -> Final Cave**.

Note: Older guides may call Ancient Armor "Golden Armor." In current progression, collect **Ancient Armor**, then upgrade/coat it with **Solafite** before opening the final gold door.

Coordinate note: Sons of the Forest's in-game GPS does not display numeric coordinates. The coordinate tables at the end use MapGenie map coordinates and marker links, which are the most practical external GPS/map reference for routing.

Visual route maps: [Open the interactive map](sons-of-the-forest-route-map.html) or [open the checklist-tracking map](sons-of-the-forest-route-checklist-map.html). Both use external MapGenie tiles and require internet access for the background map.

## Optimized Route

This reordered checklist assumes the [Beach Spawn Point](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=269023) because it gave the shortest sensible route in the coordinate pass while still keeping the progression chain practical. The order below minimizes major backtracking without turning the guide into a terrain-blind speedrun line.

## 1. Beach Spawn Open

- [ ] Build only a minimal tarp save, fire, and optional drying rack.
  - Why: you are leaving this area quickly.
- [ ] Tell Kelvin to gather sticks, fish, rocks, or logs while you loot.
  - Why: passive resources support the whole route.
- [ ] Craft `Crafted Spear`, `Crafted Bow`, `Stone Arrows`, and `Molotovs` as soon as materials allow.
  - Why: they carry the early cave chain without wasting gun ammo.
- [ ] Start collecting bones for `Bone Armor`.
  - Why: best armor economy for the whole run.
- [ ] Sweep the nearby north-beach cluster first.
- [ ] Enter [Coastal Cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268797) and collect the [Rebreather](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268850), [Stun Gun](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268867), Artifact Piece #1, cave supplies, and optional [Powered Cross blueprint](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=306326).
  - Why: Rebreather is the first hard progression lock and this cave is almost on top of the beach spawn.
- [ ] Pick up the nearby [Can Opener](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268925) after leaving the cave.
  - Why: the north-beach pickup is the shortest Can Opener option from the recommended spawn.
- [ ] Grab the [Machete](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268807) from the north beach camp immediately after.
  - Why: the Can Opener -> Machete -> inland line is slightly shorter than returning for either item later.

## 2. Inland Prep Before The West Swing

- [ ] Move inland and collect the hanging-body [Flashlight](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268784).
  - Why: you want this before the next two caves, even though delaying it slightly is technically shorter on paper.
- [ ] Continue to the [Modern Axe](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268852) camp and pick up the Modern Axe and a [Cooking Pot](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=288450).
  - Why: this is the cleanest inland power spike before committing west.
- [ ] Mark a useful [Hang Glider](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=270178) only if it is directly on your path.
  - Why: gliders are strong, but forcing extra cliffs early is slower than just running the route.

## 3. West Swing For Rope Gun

- [ ] Head west toward the Rope Gun cave and pick up the [Stun Baton](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268851) near the waterfall skull pile.
  - Why: it is almost free while crossing into the Rope Gun area.
- [ ] Enter [Ropegun Cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268823) and collect the [Cross](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268823), supplies, Artifact Piece #2, and [Rope Gun](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268866).
  - Why: Rope Gun is the second hard progression lock and this west swing is cleaner before returning to Shovel Cave.

## 4. Shovel Cave Return

- [ ] Cut back east to [Shovel Cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268811).
  - Required: `Rebreather` and `Rope Gun`.
- [ ] Pick up the [Slingshot](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268853) outside the cave.
  - Why: free pickup on entry.
- [ ] Inside, collect the [Wetsuit](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268881), [Flashlight Attachment](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268854), cave loot, and the [Shovel](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268849).
  - Why: Shovel opens the bunker network and almost every important optional weapon.

## 5. Maintenance A First

- [ ] Go directly to [Maintenance Bunker A](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268801).
  - Why: everything else gets better once you have the keycard, axe, printer, and bunker GPS clues.
- [ ] Collect the [Maintenance Keycard](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268872), [Firefighter Axe](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268868), [Pistol Rail](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=323708), and [Laser Sight](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268885).
- [ ] Print a `Flask` first.
  - Why: this saves more time over the full route than early Tech Mesh.
- [ ] Print `Printed Arrows` or `Grappling Hooks` only if resin is healthy.
- [ ] Read the laptops.
  - Why: they cleanly reveal the next bunker chain.

## 6. North Coast Weapon And Keycard Sweep

- [ ] Go to the [Food and Dining Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268800) next.
  - Why: it is the shortest sensible next stop from Maintenance A and gives major ranged power plus the next keycard.
- [ ] Collect the [Crossbow](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268889), bolts, food stockpile, and [VIP Keycard](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268888).
- [ ] Move north-west to the coast and collect the [Binoculars](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=270137).
  - Why: this is slightly shorter than doing the shotgun grave first, and both are in the same surface sweep.
- [ ] Dig the grave at the GPS marker and collect the [Shotgun](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268805).
  - Why: huge safety increase for the bunker-heavy midgame.
- [ ] Continue south-west to the raft and collect the [Pistol](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268816).
  - Why: this keeps the westward movement continuous instead of forcing a separate coastal detour later.

## 7. West Bunker Corridor

- [ ] Enter the [Entertainment Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268821).
  - Why: you are already moving west, and this unlocks the Residential chain.
- [ ] Save at the printer room if needed.
- [ ] Collect the [Guest Keycard](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268938), [Guitar](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268937), [Shotgun Rail](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268914), ammo, and [Chainsaw](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268942).
- [ ] Grab the nearby [Glider Launcher blueprint](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=323488) if you want glider utility later.
  - Why: it is closest to the Entertainment corridor in the optimized route.
- [ ] Dig and clear [Maintenance Bunker B](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268830).
  - Why: it is the cleanest nearby stop after Entertainment.
- [ ] Collect the [Compound Bow](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268870) and [Pistol Silencer](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268871).
- [ ] Swing by the [Leather Jacket](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268959) plane-crash site if you want the clothing pickup.
  - Why: this is the cheapest time to grab it.
- [ ] Continue to the southwest military camp and collect [Night Vision Goggles](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=272386).
  - Why: this is the lowest-backtracking point to pick them up before turning inland again.
- [ ] Dip into the nearby [Teleporter blueprint cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=325544).
  - Why: it is almost directly on the southwest-to-east transition and is useful for postgame mobility.

## 8. Southwest Into Endgame Unlocks

- [ ] Enter the [Residential Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268836).
  - Why: Guest Keycard now converts directly into Katana power and Luxury Bunker access.
- [ ] Collect the [Katana](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268971), [Putter](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268967), Gold Mask, ammo, supplies, and unlock the [Luxury Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268841).
- [ ] Go next to the [Ancient Armor cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268835) and collect [Ancient Armor](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268972) and the [Rifle](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=288462).
  - Why: this ordering is shorter than doing Pickaxe first and also keeps endgame prerequisites together.
- [ ] Collect both Cave D artifact pieces while clearing the Ancient Armor route.
  - Why: this avoids a late return to one of the longest caves.
- [ ] Then go to the [Pickaxe cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=304493).
  - Why: from Ancient Armor cave, this is the shortest clean continuation toward the southeast finish.
- [ ] Collect the [Pickaxe](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=304494), [Solafite Weapon Upgrader blueprint](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=306325), Artifact Piece #5, and at least 12 `Solafite Ore`.
  - Tip: 12 ore covers Ancient Armor. Mine 6 extra ore per weapon upgrade you actually intend to use.

## 9. Southeast Finish Sweep

- [ ] Dig and clear [Maintenance Bunker C](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268842).
  - Why: it is now directly on the route toward the golf-course/luxury finish.
- [ ] Collect the [Revolver](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268899).
- [ ] Pick up the [Winter Jacket](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=323698) at the northeast military tent.
  - Why: this stop is now a short eastward grab instead of a dedicated midgame detour.
- [ ] Clear [Cave G / Grind Trap Cave](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=308529) for the Grind Trap blueprint and Artifact Piece #6.
  - Why: the coordinate insertion is very cheap after Winter Jacket and before Luxury Bunker.
- [ ] Enter the [Luxury Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268841).
- [ ] Collect the [Solafite Armor Upgrader blueprint](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=323748) near the gold-door area.
- [ ] Build the upgrader somewhere defensible and upgrade/coat the `Ancient Armor`.
- [ ] Upgrade spare favorite weapons only after the armor is done.
  - Recommended order: `Katana`, `Firefighter Axe`, `Shotgun`, `Revolver`, `Pistol`.

## 10. Final Cave

- [ ] Return to the gold door in the [Luxury Bunker](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268841) with upgraded [Ancient Armor](https://mapgenie.io/sons-of-the-forest/maps/world?locationIds=268972).
  - Why: this opens the Hell Cave/final route.
- [ ] Restock before committing.
  - Bring: `Cross`, shotgun shells, pistol/revolver/rifle ammo, meds, food, water, batteries, armor, and explosives.
- [ ] Use the `Cross` aggressively against demons.
  - Why: it saves a large amount of ammo in the final section.
- [ ] Use firearms only for dangerous enemies, bosses, or emergency space-making.
- [ ] Complete the final cave.

## Fastest Progression Tips

- Use the `Beach Spawn Point` route unless you are intentionally adapting for a different start. It gave the shortest sensible coordinate route.
- Route surface items only when they are near your current cave or bunker path. Crossing the island for one minor item usually wastes more time than it saves.
- Prioritize tools over weapons early: `Flashlight`, `Rebreather`, `Rope Gun`, and `Shovel` unlock more value than extra combat gear.
- Use crafted bow, compound bow, crossbow, spear throws, and molotovs for routine fights.
- Save firearms for Fingers, Twins, Blue Puffies, bosses, demons, and panic situations.
- Burn cannibal bodies for bones and craft Bone Armor. It is the best practical armor economy.
- Creepy Armor is excellent when mutants provide it for free, but do not farm it at the cost of progression speed.
- Give Virginia the pistol or shotgun only after you are comfortable losing direct control of that weapon.
- Keep one GPS locator for a reusable glider, cave exit, or temporary loot base.
- Print `Flask` before Tech Mesh. Water convenience saves more time than early Tech Armor usually does.
- Use Night Vision Goggles and flashlight carefully unless you have spare batteries.
- Always loot green ammo boxes, printer resin, duct tape, rope, batteries, wire, circuit boards, watches, and C4.
- Read laptops as you go; they reveal bunker locations and prevent wasted searching.
- Use water against mutants when possible. Several enemies become easier to avoid or kill around deep water.
- Build Solafite upgrade structures away from your main base because activation can attract attacks.
- If a cave or bunker has a save point, use it before entering the combat-heavy section.
